Cheesy Muffins

Description

Easy to make cheese muffins the whole family can enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of self raising flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of grated cheese
  • 1 egg
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of chopped rosemary - (optional)

Steps

  1. Turn oven on to 180 degrees
  2. Mix flour, salt and rosemary in a bowl
  3. Mix in egg
  4. Add milk
  5. Add cheese, then mix
  6. Spray canola on small muffin tray (24x muffin tray)
  7. Scoop in mix
  8. Bake for 8 minutes, then turn tray around. Bake for another 6 minutes, until golden
  9. ENJOY!
