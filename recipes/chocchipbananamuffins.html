Chocolate chip banana muffins

Description

A great snack and an easy to make recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of self raising flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 egg lightly beaten
  • 4x ripe bananas
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Turn oven on to 180 degrees
  2. Melt butter
  3. Mix in brown sugar
  4. Add mashed bananas
  5. Add lightly beaten egg
  6. Mix in flour
  7. Mix in chocolate chips
  8. Spray canola on small muffin tray (24x muffin tray)
  9. Scoop in mix
  10. Bake for 8 minutes, then turn tray around. Bake for another 8 minutes, until golden
  11. ENJOY!
