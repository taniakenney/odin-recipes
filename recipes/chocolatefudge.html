Chocolate Fudge

Description

A simple, no fuss, no cook dessert.

Ingredients

  • 290g of cooking chocolate
  • 1 can of condensed milk

Steps

  1. Place chocolate and condensed milk in a glass bowl
  2. Place in microwave for 1 minute
  3. Mix well
  4. Transfer to a square tin that is lined with baking paper.
  5. Place in fridge for 1.5 hours.
  6. Once set, cut into small squares
  7. ENJOY!
